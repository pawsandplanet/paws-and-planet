Why Animal Birth Control Matters

Fewer births mean fewer lives lost. Animal Birth Control (ABC) is the most humane and effective way to manage stray populations and improve the lives of street animals. By preventing unwanted litters, we reduce suffering, disease, and conflict in overcrowded areas.
